Tracking
Logging Roads
in the
Congo Basin

The Logging Roads Initiative is a joint project of the World Resource Institute and Moabi. Our aim is to monitor geographic data on logging roads across the Congo Basin region of Central Africa. Using fifteen years of satellite imagery, the project will record the precise spread of logging roads over time.

All data will be added to OpenStreetMap and licensed in the public domain.
