Monitoring Logging in the Congo Basin

The Logging Roads project is a joint initiative of the World Resource Institute and Moabi to monitor the spread of logging roads across the Congo Basin. The project relies on open contributions from the crowd to interpret satellite imagery and track the growth of logging activity.

Anyone, anywhere, can help contribute.

All data will be added to OpenStreetMap and licensed in the public domain.
