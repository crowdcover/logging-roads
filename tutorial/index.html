How to Contribute to the Logging Roads Initiative

All logging road data are stored in OpenStreetMap, a global dataset of geographic data fully managed by crowd-based edits.

The Fix Logging Roads tool updates data in OpenStreetMap based on your responses. Anyone can participate, no previous mapping experience is required. You will need to sign up for an OpenStreetMap account using your email address. Instructions are below.

If you are familiar with OpenStreetMap, we also need help mapping roads. Click Here for more information. Also see the OSM Mapping Guide

Getting Started

Requirements: Web browser + internet connection - We recommend using either the Chrome or Firefox web browsers for this task.

Go to http://loggingroads.org, click "Start Contributing", then click on a task, for example "Tag Logging Road Creation Dates". As the project progresses additional tasks may be listed.

This will take you to the http://fix.loggingroads.org tool. This tool shows one task at a time and asks you to select the correct answer. You can preview the tasks, however to participate you will need to Login.

Login / Sign Up for an OpenStreetMap Account

Click the "Get Started" button.

A login form for OpenStreetMap will popup. (If you don't see the form you may need to allow popups)

If you don't have an account, click "register now". This will display a sign up form and then ask you to confirm your email. For more help signing up, please see http://learnosm.org/en/beginner/start-osm/#create-an-openstreetmap-account

After logging in, OpenStreetMap will ask you to authorize the Logging Roads application to use your account.

Identify the Year a Road is Created

After you login you will be shown a task. The pink road line identifies the road line for the task. We need to find the first year where the pink road line is visible in the imagery.

To make it easier to see the imagery, try turning the road lines on/off using the "Show Roads" checkbox.

Use the "More Years" button view more additional years. We currently have imagery for 2000 through 2014.

Selecting the Year

Many of the roads are extremely difficult (or impossible) to identify with the current imagery. If that is the case, please click "Skip/Too Difficult". Only select a year if the road is obviously identifiable in the imagery.

If you are able to see the year the road is created, click "Select" on that year.

You will be asked to confirm the edit being made to OpenStreetMap. Click "Save to OSM" to submit. The site will automatically show you the next task.

Viewing Your Contributions

Click the "Statistics" link to view how many contributes you've made and see where you rank with other contributors. (Note: your contributions may take 24 hours to show in the statistics.)
